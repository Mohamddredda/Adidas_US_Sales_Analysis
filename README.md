# Analyzing Adidas US Sales Performance 👟📊

I recently built an interactive Excel dashboard to analyze Adidas' sales data in the US across 2020 and 2021. Transforming raw data into a visual story helps uncover the true drivers behind revenue growth and market penetration.

Here are the key highlights from the analysis:

• The Big Picture: The data reflects a robust performance with total sales exceeding $120 Million, nearly 2.48M units sold, and a strong average operating margin of 42%.

• Omnichannel Balance: Sales methods are incredibly well-distributed among In-store (37%), Online (33%), and Outlets (30%), showcasing a highly resilient and diversified sales strategy.

• Top Performers: West Gear and Foot Locker emerged as the leading retail partners. On the product side, Men's Street Footwear is the clear top revenue generator.

• Market Trends: The geographic heat map reveals a strong market presence in key coastal and southern states, while the time-series analysis shows a massive surge in sales throughout 2021. 📈

🛠️ Skills & Tools applied: Advanced Excel, Pivot Tables, Data Modeling, Geographic Mapping, and Interactive Dashboard Design using Slicers.
